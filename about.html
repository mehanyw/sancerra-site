ABOUT US

Pioneering creative solutions to the housing shortage in California and beyond.

With extensive expertise and experience in residential and mixed-use development, we are pioneering creative, market-driven, and attainable solutions to the housing shortage in California and beyond.

OUR MISSION

At Sancerra, we don’t just build houses, we build homes. We don’t just build developments, we build community. We transform land into productive, sustainable, and engaging spaces.

OUR VISION

Through honest, collaborative, and sustainable development, we partner with communities and investors to reimagine the housing landscape.

MEET THE TEAM

With a track record of more than 175 transactions valued at over $10B, Sancerra is ready to lead.

  • Jim Chahine

    Managing Partner

    Jim has managed 61 transactions with a market value of over $3.0B. Most recently, he spent 17 years as Chief Operating Officer and Chief Financial Officer for Integral Communities and was a key member of the company's Executive Management team, with direct responsibility for accounting, finance, forecasting, strategic planning, legal, investor relations, acquisitions, dispositions and partnership compliance. Jim has also held leadership positions with Pulte Homes, Ryland Homes, and Ernest & Young, and has served on the Board of Directors for the Make a Wish Foundation and Thomas House Family Shelter. Jim graduated with a BS in Business Administration from the University of Michigan.

  • Marcus Cook

    Managing Partner

    Marcus has managed 37 transactions with a market value of over $2.7B. Marcus spent 7 years as Head of Acquisitions for Irvine Company, where he was responsible for acquisitions, dispositions, due diligence, development planning, market research, and analytics across the company’s 5 product lines. Marcus has also held positions of leadership with Integral Communities, MBK Homes, Larwin Homes, and Bank of America. He serves on the Executive Committee for USC's Lusk Center of Real Estate, and spent 5 years as an adjunct professor for USC's Master of Real Estate Development (MRED) program. Marcus graduated with BS in Business Administration from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and a Master of Real Estate Development from the University of Southern California.

  • Mike Rios

    Partner

    Mike has managed 52 transactions with a market value of over $5.0B. Mike was previously Chief Investment Officer at Lyon Living, leading a team responsible for the acquisition, capitalization, asset management and disposition of assets. Mike also served as Vice President at Alvarez & Marsal and Lehman Brothers Holdings Inc., where he managed a $2.0B portfolio of distressed commercial real estate investments on behalf of the bankrupt Lehman Brothers estate. Mike began his career with leadership positions at Goldman Sachs and Credit Suisse. He graduated with a BS in Business Administration from Pace University in New York.

  • Adam Smith

    Partner

    Adam has managed 26 transactions and the acquisition or development of over 8,000 residential units. He is the former Head of Development at Lyon Living, where he was responsible for leading a team tasked with the due diligence, pre-development, and entitlement of the company's most complex multifamily and mixed-use assets. Adam was also a founding member and Vice President at CV Communities (a division of City Ventures), where he managed the acquisition, entitlement, asset management, and disposition of distressed residential land assets. Adam began his career with leadership positions at Standard Pacific Homes and Boeing. He graduated with a BS in Mechanical Engineering from UC Santa Barbara and earned his MS in Mechanical Engineering from Stanford University.

  • Scott Covington

    Director

    Scott’s career began as a tenant representation broker for the Staubach Company and later a land broker for the Hoffman Company. He has held land acquisition leadership roles at DR Horton and Warmington Residential, and has been responsible for the entitlement, acquisition, and disposition of over 1200 units/lots across Orange, Los Angeles, Ventura and Riverside Counties. He maintained responsibilities ranging from deal sourcing, market analysis, transactional review, and project management. Prior to his real estate career, Scott played at quarterback for the St. Louis Rams, Cincinnati Bengals, and Kansas City Chiefs. He graduated with a BS in Business Administration from the University of Miami.

  • Jake Lemmerman

    Manager

    Jake previously worked at Taco Bell Corporate, where he served in various roles in Finance, Development, and Franchise Strategy. He was responsible for sourcing, underwriting, and managing the development of over 100 combined restaurants, totaling over $200M in revenue. Additionally, he supported all franchisees west of the Mississippi on their financial health, acquisitions and dispositions, and asset strategy. He was also responsible for managing the company’s multi-million-dollar corporate development capital account and new development pipeline. Prior to his career in real estate development, Jake was a professional baseball player with the Los Angeles Dodgers, St. Louis Cardinals, and San Diego Padres. He graduated with a BS in History and earned his certificate in Markets and Management from Duke University.
