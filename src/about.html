ABOUT US

Pioneering creative solutions to the housing shortage in California and beyond.

With extensive expertise and experience in residential and mixed-use development, we are pioneering creative, market-driven, and attainable solutions to the housing shortage in California and beyond.

OUR MISSION

At Sancerra we don't just build houses, we build communities. We don’t just build developments, we build neighborhoods. We transform land into productive, sustainable, and engaging spaces.

OUR VISION

Through honest, collaborative, and sustainable development, we partner with communities and investors to reimagine the housing landscape.

MEET THE TEAM

With a track record of more than 175 transactions valued at over $10 billion, Sancerra is leading the industry through the housing shortage.

  • Jim Chahine, CPA

    Managing Partner

    Jim is the Co-Founder of Sancerra and has been instrumental in managing over 60 transactions with a market value of over $3.0B. Most recently, he spent 17 years as Chief Operating Officer and Chief Financial Officer for Integral Communities and was a key member of the company's Executive Management team, with direct responsibility for accounting, finance, forecasting, strategic planning, legal, investor relations, acquisitions, dispositions and partnership compliance. Jim has also held leadership positions with Pulte Homes, Ryland Homes, and Ernest & Young, and has served on the Board of Directors for the Make a Wish Foundation and Thomas House Family Shelter. Jim graduated with a BS in Business Administration from the University of Michigan.

  • Marcus Cook, CFA

    Managing Partner

    Marcus brings more than 30 years of real estate and investment experience to the Sancerra team. Mr. Cook is a Co-Founder of Sancerra and has managed nearly 40 transactions with a market value of over $2.7B.
